## Migration Principles

1. **Export before anything**: Always download a full data export before making changes
2. **Mine before archive**: Extract insights from every chat/project before closing it
3. **Test with low stakes first**: The "Play Acting Piper Morgan" test migration validates the process before touching active projects
4. **Five-layer fidelity check**: Use our documented import fidelity methodology (from Archie's exploration) to verify each migration
5. **One account at a time**: Don't try to consolidate everything at once
6. **VA boundary decision last**: Don't force the VA decision until Phases 1-2 are done — the experience will inform it
