# Chat Session Log
**Project**: Website Redesign for Local Coffee Shop  
**Started**: June 24, 2025  
**Status**: In Progress

## Current Objective
Design a modern, mobile-first website for "Bean There" coffee shop that increases online orders and showcases their local roasting operation.

## Key Decisions Made
- **Tech Stack**: Chose WordPress over custom build (6/24) - client needs easy content updates
- **Design Direction**: Went with warm, rustic aesthetic over minimalist approach (6/24) - better fits brand personality  
- **Priority Features**: Online ordering system takes precedence over blog functionality (6/24)

## Lessons Learned
- Client initially wanted "everything" but budget reality check focused scope on essentials
- Their current site analytics show 70% mobile traffic - confirms mobile-first approach
- Store owner is not tech-savvy - simple CMS crucial for long-term success
