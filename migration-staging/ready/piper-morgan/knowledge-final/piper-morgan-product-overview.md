# Piper Morgan Product Overview

## Vision
An AI-powered Product Manager assistant that understands PM workflows, automates routine tasks, and provides intelligent support for strategic decisions.

## Target Users

### Primary Personas
- **Overwhelmed PM**: Product Managers drowning in administrative tasks who need to reclaim time for strategic thinking
- **Scaling PM Lead**: PM team leads wanting to establish consistent processes across their growing team
- **Solo PM**: Individual PMs at startups wearing multiple hats who need force multiplication

### User Pain Points
- Spending 40% of time on admin tasks (status updates, meeting notes, ticket grooming)
- Context switching between multiple projects loses critical details
- Inconsistent processes across PM team members
- Difficulty maintaining institutional knowledge as team scales
- Repetitive stakeholder communications eating into deep work time

## Core Capabilities

### Currently Implemented
- Daily standup facilitation and priority setting
- Bug triage and severity assessment
- Feature specification assistance
- Stakeholder communication drafting
- Sprint planning support
- Knowledge management across projects
- GitHub issue creation and tracking
- Slack integration for team updates

### Planned Enhancements
- Jira integration for enterprise teams
- Custom workflow learning from team patterns
- Predictive risk identification
- Automated status report generation
- Meeting transcript analysis and action item extraction

## Key Differentiators

### vs. Generic AI Assistants (ChatGPT, Claude)
- **PM-Specific Context**: Understands PM terminology, workflows, and priorities
- **Persistent Memory**: Maintains context across sessions and projects
- **Tool Integration**: Native connections to PM tools, not just chat
- **Workflow Automation**: Executes tasks, doesn't just advise

### vs. Traditional PM Tools (Jira, Asana)
- **Intelligent Assistance**: Makes recommendations, not just tracking
- **Natural Language**: No complex query languages or rigid forms
- **Cross-Tool Orchestration**: Works across your entire tool stack
- **Adaptive Learning**: Improves with your team's specific patterns

## Success Metrics

### User Success
- Time saved per PM per week (target: 8 hours)
- Reduction in context switching penalties
- Improvement in stakeholder satisfaction scores
- Decrease in dropped balls/missed items

### Business Success
- User retention (target: >80% monthly active)
- Expansion within organizations (team adoption)
- Reduction in user's tool stack costs
- NPS score from PM community

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors
- **ProductBoard AI**: Strong roadmapping, weak execution support
- **Notion AI**: Good documentation, lacks PM-specific workflows
- **Linear Insights**: Developer-focused, less PM-centric

### Indirect Competitors
- Generic AI assistants with PM prompts
- Traditional PM tools with bolt-on AI features
- Consultants and PM coaches

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Individual PM Adoption
- Free tier for individual PMs
- Focus on immediate time-saving wins
- Build community and gather feedback

### Phase 2: Team Expansion
- Team features and shared knowledge base
- Integration with existing tool stack
- Success stories and case studies

### Phase 3: Enterprise Scale
- Advanced security and compliance
- Custom workflow configuration
- Professional services for implementation
