Q&A Prep

Q&A Preparation

Anticipated Questions & Framework Answers:

Q: "How do I convince my org to let me experiment with AI coding?"

A: Frame it as extending design impact, not changing careers. Start with systematic prototyping for user testing—show them you can get from design to testable prototype in hours instead of weeks.

Q: "What if I'm not technical enough for this?"

A: Your IA background gives you systematic thinking foundations. Start with verification-first approaches you already know—research existing patterns before creating new ones.

Q: "Won't this make UX jobs obsolete?"

A: The opposite. AI democratizes outputs but increases need for systematic human advocacy and strategic thinking. Someone needs to ensure all these AI-generated interfaces actually serve human needs.

Q: "Which AI tools should I start with?"

A: Focus on tools that integrate with your existing workflow (Figma AI, content research AI) and apply systematic verification first. Check what patterns already exist before creating new ones.

Voice: Keep answers practical and encouraging. Always connect back to their existing expertise.
