## The Anti-Flattening Protocol

### Problem Statement
When we created canonical queries, the consciousness model got flattened from "embodied AI self-awareness" to "static capability lists." We MUST prevent this from happening to the UX 2.0 vision.

### Solution: Three-Layer Issue Structure

Each issue contains:
1. **VISION** - The conceptual "why" with citations
2. **SPECIFICATION** - The concrete "what" 
3. **VERIFICATION** - How we know it's not flattened
