## Usage Notes

1. **Start with Core Profile** - Always fill out the base template
2. **Add Role Extensions** - Append relevant sections based on their role(s)
3. **Multiple Roles are Normal** - Ted is Advisor + Alpha Tester + (future) Contributor
4. **Update Regularly** - Refresh after significant interactions
5. **Link to Artifacts** - Reference specific docs, issues, transcripts
6. **Capture Communication Style** - This helps anyone who needs to interact with them

---

## Role Definitions

| Role | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Alpha Tester** | Testing the software, reporting bugs, providing UX feedback |
| **Advisor** | Providing expertise and guidance on architecture, methodology, domain |
| **Contributor** | Writing code, docs, or other artifacts that get merged into the project |
| **Collaborator** | Working together on shared goals, may have parallel projects |
| **Amplifier** | Has platform/audience and shares Piper Morgan story externally |

A person's role can evolve over time (Alpha Tester â†’ Contributor) or expand (Advisor adds Alpha Tester).
