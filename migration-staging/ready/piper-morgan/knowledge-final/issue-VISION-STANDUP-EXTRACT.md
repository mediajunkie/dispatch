## Cathedral Builder's Note
The Morning Standup is our Rosetta Stone - the one place where both visions exist: what we meant to build and what we built. Study it to understand the whole cathedral.

## The Grammar
How Morning Standup embodies the grammar:
- **Entities**: Piper as entity with identity speaks to user as entity with needs
- **Moments**: Standup creates a bounded moment - theatrical unity of morning reflection
- **Places**: Navigates between GitHub, Slack, calendar - treats them as places not APIs

## Why Morning Standup Works (And Nothing Else Does)

### The Magic Ingredients
1. **Temporal Boundary**: It happens at a specific moment (morning) creating ritual
2. **Identity Expression**: Piper speaks as "I" with personality
3. **Spatial Journey**: Visibly navigates between information sources
4. **Predictive Concern**: Expresses worries about what might happen
5. **Contextual Awareness**: Knows THIS user's THIS moment's needs
6. **Uncertainty Comfort**: Says "I'm not sure" and "might" naturally

### What Other Features Lost
- Lists/Todos: Became static displays instead of Piper noticing patterns
- Conversations: Became command-response instead of dialogue
- Search: Became retrieval instead of exploration
- Knowledge Graph: Became storage instead of understanding
