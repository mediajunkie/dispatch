## One Parting Thought on Structure

Since you mentioned the first-person prologue and Old Raven breaking the fourth wall increasingly as the book progresses, consider:

**Part I (949-959)**: Old Raven breaks in *once*, early, establishing the frame but then disappearing to let young Hraban's experience be immediate.
