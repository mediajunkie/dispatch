## Daily Schedule

**Dawn**: Wakes, morning prayers (mixture of Latin and Greek now)
**Early Morning**: Simple breakfast - bread, watered wine, cheese
**Third Hour (9 AM)**: Reports to library/scriptorium for work
**Morning-Midday**: Main work period

- Translating documents
- Authentication tasks

**Afternoon**: Continue work, or attend palace ceremonies if needed
**Late Afternoon**: His "walks" - exploring Constantinople, processing thoughts
**Evening**: Study time - Greek texts, practice languages
**Night**: Dinner with other clerks/students, early to bed (dawn comes early)

## Physical Appearance (Age 17-18)

**Build**: Coltish, slender, just shedding baby fat
**Face**: Not quite a man yet, minimal facial hair (less than Greek/Armenian peers)
**Hair**: Dark (hence "Hraban" = raven), kept longer in Byzantine style
**Eyes**: Intense, dark, observant - "watching like his namesake"
**Distinguishing Feature**: Withered left arm
- Shortened, limited mobility
- Twisted fingers curled in permanent half-fist
- He's learned to use it to hold parchment while writing
- No longer hides it as much as in childhood

**Typical Dress** (Mid-level Clerk):
- **Chiton**: Simple tunic, undyed or earth tones (brown, dark green, dark blue)

## The Fibula (His Talisman)

**Description**: Small bronze or silver cloak pin (fastens his chlamys)
**Origin**: His mother's, given when he left for Constantinople
**Design**: Germanic/Saxon style with runic markings
**Modification**: Cross carved over the pagan runes (conversion marker)
**Condition**: Worn smooth from constant handling

**How He Uses It**:
- Palms it in his left hand when anxious
- Sharp pin pricks his palm - pain brings focus
- Holds it tight in twisted fist during stressful moments
- Touches it for reassurance before difficult tasks
- Physical anchor when his mind wanders into counting/language labyrinths

**Symbolic Significance**:
- Germanic heritage literally overwritten (like his identity)

- Mother's gift (emotional anchor to home)
