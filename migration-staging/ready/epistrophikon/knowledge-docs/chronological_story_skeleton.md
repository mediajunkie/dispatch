## STRUCTURAL OBSERVATIONS

### Natural Three-Act Structure Emerges:

**ACT I: SEDUCTION (949-955)** - Ages 11-17
- Arrival through Identity Crisis
- Ends with "Weight of Purple" vault scene
- The question posed

**ACT II: TRANSFORMATION (955-969)** - Ages 17-31  
- Recovery through Assassination
- The long answer taking shape through experience
- Culminates in ultimate Roman violence

**ACT III: SYNTHESIS (969-972)** - Ages 31-34
- The answer lived and transmitted
- Return journey completing the circle
- Resolution: identity as choice
