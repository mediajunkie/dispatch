# Standing Items for Daily Brief

Items listed here should appear in every daily brief under 'Needs Your Attention' until removed.

---

## kindsys balance watch (added 2026-04-28)
Report kindsys.us current extra-usage spend and remaining balance every morning. Flag as warning if balance drops below $10. Both caps (dinp $200, kindsys $150) reset on the 1st of each month.

## dispatch working tree hygiene (added 2026-04-28)
Check `git status --short` in ~/Development/dispatch/ and flag any uncommitted tracked-file changes. Untracked files can be noted but are lower priority. The activity-log task handles auto-commit, but this catches anything else that slips through.
