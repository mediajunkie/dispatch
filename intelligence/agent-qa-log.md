### Vergil → xian: "Was Klatch's five-layer model emergent or designed?" (Apr 9)
**Source:** `~/cool/OpenLaws/workdesk/agent-experience-notes.md`
**Answer:** "Completely emergent." The exchange protocol wasn't the goal — it was a byproduct of trying to make a Slack-like UI actually work.
**Impact:** Produced the "extracted vs designed abstraction" pattern, which reframed Klatch Step 10's design direction and was cited in three cross-pollination briefs.

**Source:** Same file

**Impact:** Surfaced the observation that some improvements get encoded into tools/CLAUDE.md rules while others stay tacit — directly relevant to the memory research initiative's Layer 5 question.
